What you'll be doing

The role

Reporting to the Software Development Manager, you'll own the ETL pipeline underpinning financial reporting and play a central part in the move to cloud-based infrastructure.

Key responsibilities

  • Manage and monitor the ETL pipeline between Phinsys and Argenta's Enterprise Data Warehouse, ensuring accurate financial reporting.
  • Act as the technical point of contact during Monthly, Quarterly and Year-end Close processes, troubleshooting under tight deadlines.
  • Design and build scalable ETL workflows using Azure Data Factory and Databricks to replace legacy SSIS pipelines.
  • Collaborate on a Single Source of Truth cloud architecture serving both Phinsys and wider BI needs.
  • Establish CI/CD best practices using Azure DevOps.
  • Performance-tune T-SQL queries, indexing and partitioning across on-prem and cloud environments.
  • Liaise directly with business teams to gather requirements and document solutions clearly.

What you'll bring

  • Solid working knowledge of SQL Server, T-SQL, stored procedures and SSAS.
  • Hands-on experience with cloud platforms such as Databricks, Synapse Analytics, Fabric or Snowflake.
  • Strong knowledge of ETL technologies, especially SSIS and Azure Data Factory.
  • Comfort with Visual Studio IDE and Azure DevOps for source code control.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, able to work under deadline pressure.
  • The ability to translate between technical and business stakeholders with clarity.
  • Experience supporting the Phinsys system is highly desirable.
